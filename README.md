# FiscalFlow: Master Your Money

FiscalFlow is a modern, intuitive, and AI-powered personal finance application designed to help you take control of your financial life. It provides a comprehensive suite of tools to track your spending, manage budgets, set savings goals, and gain intelligent insights into your financial habits. The application is fully responsive and supports multiple languages.

## Key Features

### 1. Seamless Onboarding & Setup
- **Simple Login**: Get started in seconds by just providing your name and age. No complex sign-up process required.
- **Guided Budget Setup**: For new users, a simple two-step wizard helps you set up your initial monthly budget. Just enter your salary, and the app will guide you to allocate it across different spending categories. You can also skip this and set it up later.

### 2. Comprehensive Dashboard
The dashboard provides an at-a-glance overview of your entire financial picture.
- **Financial Summary Cards**: Key metrics like Total Income, Total Expenses, Total Savings, and Net Balance are displayed prominently.
- **Spending Overview Chart**: A visual bar chart breaks down your spending by category, helping you quickly identify where your money is going.
- **Recent Transactions**: A live feed of your most recent transactions.
- **Budget Progress**: See how you're tracking against your monthly budgets for each category with clear progress bars.

### 3. Smart Transaction Management
Log and categorize every transaction with ease.
- **Add, Edit, & Delete**: A full-featured system to manage your financial records.
- **Mobile-First Design**: On mobile, transactions are displayed in a clean, stacked card list. On desktop, they appear in a powerful data table with sorting and filtering capabilities.
- **AI-Powered Bill Scanning**: Use your phone's camera or upload a photo of a receipt, and our AI will automatically extract the vendor, amount, date, and suggest a category for the transaction.
- **Flexible Transaction Types**: Classify transactions as **Income**, **Expense**, or **Saving** to get a clear picture of your cash flow.

### 4. Intelligent Budgeting
- **Manage Monthly Budgets**: Set spending limits for each category on the dedicated Budgets page.
- **Track Spending in Real-Time**: The app shows you how much you've spent against your budget for each category.
- **AI-Powered Recommendations**: Get smart suggestions from an AI financial advisor on how to adjust your budgets based on your spending patterns to curb overspending.

### 5. Goal-Oriented Savings
- **Set Financial Goals**: Create, track, and manage your savings goals, whether it's for a new laptop, a vacation, or a down payment.
- **Add & Remove Funds**: Easily allocate money to your goals or withdraw funds when needed.
- **Add Leftover Balance**: A smart feature allows you to add your leftover monthly balance to a goal with a single click.

### 6. In-Depth Reporting
Analyze your financial data to make informed decisions.
- **Custom Date Ranges**: Filter your reports to view data for this month, last month, or the last three months.
- **Spending by Category**: A vertical bar chart shows a detailed breakdown of expenses across different categories.
- **Category Breakdown**: A pie chart visualizes the percentage of your spending for each category.
- **Income vs. Expense**: A clear bar chart compares your total income against your total expenses for the selected period.

### 7. Personalization & Settings
- **Manage Categories**: Add your own custom spending categories or remove ones you don't use.
- **Language Support**: The app is bilingual and supports both **English** and **Hindi (हिन्दी)**.
- **Data Persistence**: All your data—transactions, budgets, goals, and settings—is securely saved in your browser's local storage, so you never lose your progress.

FiscalFlow is your all-in-one solution for mastering your money with clarity, intelligence, and ease.
